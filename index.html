eggs are one of the faviourite food . here a reciepe for delicious rich Scrambled eggs .which is a popular breakfast dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them in a pan with butter or oil until they are set.
Salt, pepper, and other seasonings can be added to enhance the flavor, and various toppings such as cheese, herbs, or vegetables can also be included. Scrambled eggs are a quick and easy meal that can be enjoyed any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. melt butter in fring pan over a median heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and the cream in a boul
  3. once the butter is melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edges of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat .
